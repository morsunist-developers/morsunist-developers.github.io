What is the Morsunist Project?

Morsunist Project is a multi-purpose discord bot aimed to server management and spreading of morsunism.
Bot was created in December 20, 2018 and is supported to this day. Initially, it was made by one person, but other people came over time.

What features does it have?

  1. Play your favourite music.
  2. Search infos about your favourite anime or manga.
  3. Multi-lang supported! Change bot language to your native.
  4. Economy system. Get money and buy more kebab.
  5. Level system & profile card. Say more and be morsunist.
  6. Don't trust seals!
  7. Games! Play minesweeper, rock&paper&scissors, school shooting or bomb defusing with others or alone.
  8. Roleplay commands.
  9. Moderation commands. Kick seal from your server.
  10. Miscellaneous commands. Like image-manipulation and etc.
